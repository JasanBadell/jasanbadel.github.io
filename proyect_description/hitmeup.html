About HitMeUp

HitMeUp is a modern and user-friendly chat application that allows you to communicate with friends and family in real-time. The app makes the process of sending text messages and images fast and easy.

HitMeUp uses the powerful ChatEngine API, ensuring stability and quality of communication. Chats are stored in the cloud so that you can access your conversations from any device.

With HitMeUp, you can connect with friends and family in an easy and fun way. Download the app today and start chatting with your loved ones anytime and anywhere.
